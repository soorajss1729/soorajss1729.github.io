About Me

I am a passionate researcher in the field of theoretical quantum computing. My interests span quantum walks, simplicial complexes, and topological data analysis. I actively engage in collaborative projects to expand the horizons of quantum computing and bring novel solutions to emerging challenges.

Before diving into quantum computing, I explored advanced linear algebra and theoretical computer science. My journey has been fueled by curiosity and a drive to tackle complex problems in innovative ways.
